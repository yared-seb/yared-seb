About Us

Founded in 1973 EC (circa 1980 GC), Yaberus Wolkite General Secondary and Preparatory School is a public high school located in Wolkite, Gurage Zone, Ethiopia. The school is committed to delivering free, high-quality education that nurtures students for academic success and community leadership.

Mission

To provide inclusive, high-quality education that cultivates academic excellence, personal integrity, and lifelong learning among students.

Vision

To be a nationally recognized center of academic excellence that produces competent, responsible, and visionary leaders.

Core Values

  • Excellence: Pursuit of high standards in all aspects.
  • Integrity: Honesty and transparency in conduct.
  • Inclusivity: Equal opportunity for every student.
  • Community: Service, empathy, and collaboration.
  • Lifelong Learning: Growth mindset and curiosity.

Educational Focus

The school offers general secondary and preparatory education aimed at preparing students for university-level study and future careers. Emphasis is placed on academic rigor, character development, and community service.
